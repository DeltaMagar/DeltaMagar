Welcome to Our Homestay

Experience tranquility and comfort at Dholimara Homestay. Enjoy a peaceful stay surrounded by nature.

Welcome to Dholimara Homestay, an eco-friendly retreat nestled in the Magar community village.
Surrounded by lush greenery and tranquil landscapes, our homestay offers a unique blend of comfort and cultural immersion.
Experience the rich heritage of the Magar community through guided nature walks, traditional farming activities, and local hospitality.
Our cozy rooms are designed for relaxation, providing modern amenities while maintaining rustic charm. Escape the hustle and reconnect with nature at Dholimara Homestay, your serene sanctuary.
